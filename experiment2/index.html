Technical

Relation to inspring image:

  • Color: The sky, grass/hill, tree, treeline, and sunset colors were sampled from the inspiring image using a color picker webiste which takes the colors codes of a given image.
  • Primitives: The trees are rendered with triangle primitives.
  • Polygons: The hills and treeline were made using vertex calls
  • Ellipses: The clouds were made using layered ellipses of varying colors.
  • Composition: Different random seed values lead to different shapes of the hills and treeline and different postions of the clouds and trees.
  • Life: The postion of the clouds is animated to simulate natural cloud movement in response to the horizontal motion of the mouse.

Reflection

This experiment helped me better understand javascript. I now have a clear grasp of the screen as making ther vertices of the different terrain forced me to figure out the x and y coordinates of the generative image. Overall I had fun recreating the image and seeing its generative aspect with the random() function at use.
